I get emerged in it so badly, that everything else nearby seems to have been stopped. Very filmy, I know, but it’s the reality...    Questions arrive, “What happened to you?” Now what should I reply. They predict that some matter is hovering negative vibes inside me. The reality is that I am quiet not because of any sadness climbing my heart but due to certain facts that I am presently thinking about, and let me tell, that they are certainly not making me sad, rather they are passing some hidden smiles. I don’t want variety of tags in this matter thus I never utter anything substantial in this matter. But non-utterances don’t help anyhow, however they force me to submerge more deeply in the previous thoughts...    What I think is indeed spineless but it is surely very soothing.
